Create your own storefront to start selling products on the marketplace. When you sign up, you'll automatically get a unique subdomain for your store.

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- A unique username for your store

## Creating your account and storefront

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to the sign-up page">
  Go to the marketplace homepage and click **Sign up** in the top right corner.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter your username">
  Choose a unique username for your store. This will become your store's subdomain.
  
  For example, if you enter `pinnacle`, your storefront will be accessible at:
  ```
  pinnacle.shop.com
  ```
  
  The platform will show you a preview of your store URL as you type.
</Step>

<Step title="Complete your registration details">
  Fill in the required information:
  
  - **Username**: Your store's unique identifier (used for the subdomain)
  - **Email**: Your contact email address
  - **Password**: A secure password for your account
  
  Click **Create account** to complete the registration.
</Step>

<Step title="Access your storefront">
  After successful registration, you'll be automatically logged in. Your storefront is now created with:
  
  - A unique subdomain URL
  - An empty product catalog ready for your products
  - Access to the vendor dashboard
</Step>
</Steps>

## What happens during sign-up

When you create an account, the platform automatically:

1. Creates a tenant (your storefront) with your chosen username as the store name and URL slug
2. Sets up a Stripe account ID for future payment processing
3. Links your user account to your storefront
4. Logs you in so you can start managing your store

## Customizing your storefront

You can customize your storefront by:

- **Store Image**: Upload a logo or banner image to personalize your store
- **Store Name**: While your username is the default, you can update your store's display name
- **Slug**: Your unique subdomain identifier (set during registration)

All customization can be done through the vendor dashboard.

## Storefront visibility

Once created, your storefront is immediately visible to buyers at your unique subdomain. However, it will appear empty until you:

1. Complete Stripe Connect setup
2. Add your first products

Buyers can browse your store, view your store name and image, but won't see any products until you add them.
