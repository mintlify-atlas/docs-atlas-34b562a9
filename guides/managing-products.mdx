Manage your product catalog through the vendor dashboard. Add new products, update existing ones, and control what buyers see in your storefront.

## Prerequisites

- An active storefront account
- Stripe details submitted (required before creating products)

<Warning>
  You must complete your Stripe Connect setup and submit your payment details before you can create products.
</Warning>

## Adding a new product

<Steps>
<Step title="Access the admin dashboard">
  Navigate to the admin panel at `/admin` to access your vendor dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Go to Products collection">
  In the admin sidebar, click on **Products** to view your product catalog.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a new product">
  Click **Create New** to open the product creation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in product details">
  Enter your product information:
  
  **Required fields:**
  - **Name**: The product title that buyers will see
  - **Price**: Product price in INR (Indian Rupees)
  
  **Optional fields:**
  - **Description**: Detailed description of your product
  - **Category**: Select a category to help buyers find your product
  - **Tags**: Add multiple tags for better product discovery
  - **Image**: Upload a product image
  - **Refund Policy**: Choose your refund window
</Step>

<Step title="Set your refund policy">
  Choose from the available refund policy options:
  
  - 30-day money back guarantee (default)
  - 14-day money back guarantee
  - 7-day money back guarantee
  - 3-day money back guarantee
  - 1-day money back guarantee
  - No refunds
  
  This policy will be displayed on the product page to buyers.
</Step>

<Step title="Save and publish">
  Click **Save** to publish your product. It will immediately appear in your storefront.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Product fields explained

### Name
The product name appears on product cards, product pages, and in search results. Keep it clear and descriptive.

### Description
Provide detailed information about your product. If no description is provided, the product page will show "No description provided" to buyers.

### Price
Enter the price in Indian Rupees (INR). The platform automatically formats and displays the price to buyers using the currency symbol.

### Category
Select a category to organize your products. Categories can have subcategories, helping buyers navigate to your products through the marketplace hierarchy.

### Tags
Add multiple tags to improve product discoverability. Tags help buyers filter and find products that match their interests.

### Image
Upload a product image to make your listing more attractive. Products without images will display a placeholder.

**Image recommendations:**
- Use high-quality images
- Recommended aspect ratio: 3.9:1 for product detail pages
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG

### Refund Policy
Your chosen refund policy appears prominently on the product detail page. Choose a policy that balances buyer confidence with your business needs.

## Editing existing products

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Products">
  In the admin dashboard, go to the **Products** collection.
</Step>

<Step title="Select a product">
  Click on the product you want to edit from the product list.
</Step>

<Step title="Update fields">
  Modify any product fields you want to change.
</Step>

<Step title="Save changes">
  Click **Save** to update the product. Changes appear immediately on your storefront.
</Step>
</Steps>

## How products appear to buyers

Your products are displayed to buyers in several places:

### Product cards
In product listings, buyers see:
- Product image or placeholder
- Product name
- Price with currency formatting
- Add to cart button

### Product detail page
When buyers click on a product, they see:
- Large product image (3.9:1 aspect ratio)
- Product name and description
- Price in a highlighted badge
- Your store name and logo (clickable link to your storefront)
- Star ratings display
- Refund policy information
- Add to cart button
- Share link button

### Your storefront
Products appear in your tenant's dedicated storefront at `[your-username].shop.com`, where buyers can:
- Browse all your products
- Filter by tags and price
- Sort products
- View product details

## Organizing products with categories and tags

### Categories
Categories create a hierarchical structure:
- **Parent categories**: Top-level product groupings
- **Subcategories**: More specific classifications within a parent category

Buyers can navigate through category and subcategory pages to discover products.

### Tags
Tags provide flexible, multi-dimensional classification:
- Products can have multiple tags
- Buyers can filter products by tags
- Tags work alongside categories for better product discovery

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Write clear product names">
    Use descriptive names that clearly communicate what you're selling. Avoid excessive capitalization or special characters.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Provide detailed descriptions">
    Even though descriptions are optional, always provide them. Detailed descriptions help buyers make informed decisions and reduce refund requests.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use high-quality images">
    Products with professional images sell better. Ensure your images are clear, well-lit, and showcase your product effectively.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Choose appropriate categories">
    Select categories that accurately represent your products to help buyers find them through browse navigation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Set realistic refund policies">
    Balance buyer confidence with your business constraints. Longer refund windows can increase buyer trust.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use tags strategically">
    Add relevant tags that buyers might search for or filter by. Don't over-tag with irrelevant terms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
