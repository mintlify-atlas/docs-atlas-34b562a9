Set up Stripe Connect to receive payments when buyers purchase your products. This is a required step before you can create and sell products on the marketplace.

## Prerequisites

- An active storefront account
- Business information for Stripe verification
- Bank account details for receiving payouts

## Why Stripe Connect is required

The marketplace uses Stripe Connect to:

- Process payments securely from buyers
- Transfer funds directly to your bank account
- Handle refunds according to your refund policies
- Provide transaction reporting and analytics
- Ensure compliance with payment regulations

<Info>
  Each vendor has their own Stripe Connected Account, ensuring you receive payments directly and maintain control over your funds.
</Info>

## Understanding your Stripe account

When you create a storefront, the platform automatically:

1. Creates a Stripe Account ID for your tenant
2. Sets the `stripeDetailsSubmitted` flag to `false`
3. Blocks product creation until Stripe details are submitted

### Stripe fields in your tenant

Your tenant record includes:

- **stripeAccountId**: Your unique Stripe Connected Account identifier (read-only)
- **stripeDetailsSubmitted**: Indicates whether you've completed Stripe onboarding (read-only)

<Warning>
  You cannot create products until `stripeDetailsSubmitted` is `true`. The admin dashboard will show: "You cannot create products until you submit your Stripe details."
</Warning>

## Setting up Stripe Connect

<Steps>
<Step title="Access your vendor dashboard">
  Navigate to the admin panel at `/admin` and log in with your credentials.
</Step>

<Step title="Go to account settings">
  Look for your tenant settings or store configuration section in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Initiate Stripe Connect onboarding">
  Click on the **Connect Stripe** or **Set up payments** button to begin the Stripe Connect onboarding flow.
  
  This will redirect you to Stripe's secure onboarding portal.
</Step>

<Step title="Complete Stripe onboarding">
  Provide the required information in Stripe's onboarding form:
  
  **Business information:**
  - Business type (individual, company, etc.)
  - Business name and address
  - Tax identification information
  
  **Personal information:**
  - Your name and date of birth
  - Contact information
  - Identification documents (if required)
  
  **Bank account details:**
  - Bank account number
  - Routing number
  - Account holder name
</Step>

<Step title="Submit your details">
  Review all information and submit your Stripe Connect application.
  
  Stripe may require additional verification steps depending on your business type and location.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify completion">
  After successful submission, you'll be redirected back to the marketplace dashboard.
  
  The `stripeDetailsSubmitted` flag will now be set to `true`, and you can start creating products.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What information Stripe requires

Stripe Connect requires different information based on your business type:

### For individuals
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Home address
- Social Security Number or Tax ID
- Government-issued ID (in some cases)
- Bank account information

### For businesses
- Business legal name
- Business type and structure
- Business address
- Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- Business representative information
- Ownership information (for certain business types)
- Bank account information

<Note>
  Requirements may vary by country and region. Stripe will guide you through the specific requirements for your location.
</Note>

## After Stripe setup

Once your Stripe details are submitted:

### You can now:
- Create and publish products
- Receive payments from buyers
- View transaction history in Stripe dashboard
- Manage refunds and disputes
- Receive automatic payouts to your bank account

### Payment flow:
1. Buyer adds your products to cart and checks out
2. Buyer enters payment information
3. Stripe processes the payment
4. Funds are transferred to your Stripe Connected Account
5. Stripe automatically pays out to your linked bank account

## Managing your Stripe account

### Accessing Stripe dashboard
You can access your Stripe dashboard directly at [dashboard.stripe.com](https://dashboard.stripe.com) to:

- View detailed transaction history
- Manage refunds and disputes
- Update bank account information
- Download financial reports
- Configure payout schedules
- Update business information

### Payout schedule
By default, Stripe transfers funds to your bank account on a rolling basis:

- Standard payout timing varies by country
- You can customize payout frequency in Stripe dashboard
- View pending and completed payouts in Stripe

### Updating information
If you need to update your business or bank account information:

1. Log in to your Stripe dashboard
2. Navigate to **Settings**
3. Update the relevant information
4. Save changes

Changes to your Stripe account don't require re-onboarding in the marketplace.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't create products after Stripe setup">
    Check that:
    - You completed the entire Stripe onboarding flow
    - You submitted all required information
    - You didn't close the Stripe window before completion
    
    If the issue persists, try logging out and back in, or contact support.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stripe onboarding incomplete">
    If you closed the Stripe window before completing onboarding:
    - Return to your vendor dashboard
    - Look for a notification about incomplete Stripe setup
    - Click the link to resume onboarding
    
    You don't need to start over - Stripe saves your progress.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Need to change bank account">
    To update your bank account information:
    - Log in to your Stripe dashboard directly
    - Go to Settings → Bank accounts and scheduling
    - Update your bank account details
    
    You don't need to update anything in the marketplace dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stripe verification required">
    If Stripe requests additional verification:
    - Check your email for messages from Stripe
    - Log in to your Stripe dashboard to see verification requirements
    - Provide the requested documents or information
    
    This is a standard security measure for certain transaction volumes or business types.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security and compliance

The marketplace and Stripe ensure:

- **PCI compliance**: Buyer payment information is handled securely by Stripe
- **Data protection**: Sensitive financial data never touches marketplace servers
- **Fraud prevention**: Stripe's fraud detection protects your account
- **Regulatory compliance**: Stripe handles regional payment regulations

<Info>
  Your Stripe Account ID in the marketplace dashboard is read-only. All account management happens through Stripe's secure platform.
</Info>
